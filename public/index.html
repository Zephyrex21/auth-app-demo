  HOW IT WORKS

Your auth flow, live & explained

01

Register

Your password is hashed with bcrypt (cost=10) before storing in MongoDB. Plain text is never saved.

02

Verify & Issue JWT

On login, bcrypt compares hashes. If matched, the server signs a JWT containing your user ID & email — expires in 1 day.

03

Client-side Storage

The JWT is saved in localStorage. Explore the tradeoffs vs cookies & sessions on your dashboard.

04

Protected Routes

Every request to a guarded API sends Authorization: Bearer <token>. The middleware verifies the signature before access is granted.
